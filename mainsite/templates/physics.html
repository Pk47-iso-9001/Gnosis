    Some Definations & Laws from Heat:

  • Newton's Law of Cooling:
    • ans.It states that, "The rate of loss of heat is directly proportional to the difference of temperature of a body and its surrounding."
  • Stefan's Law of Black Body Radiation:
    • ans.It states that, "The total amount of heat energy radiated per second per unit surface area of a perfect black body is directly proportional to the fourth power of the absolute temperature of the surface of the body. "
  • Stefan-Boltzman law of Black Body Radiation:
    • ans.It states that, "The total amount of heat energy radiated per second per unit surgace area of a perfect black body is directly proportional to the difference of fourth power of absolute temperature of the body and its surroundings.
  • Relegation:
    • ans.It is the phenomenon of melting under pressure and freezing again when pressure is reduced. It was discovered by Michael Faraday.
